Voice and Tone

Voice and tone are often used interchangeably, but they are different things. Use the guide below to help with any confusion.

 

Voice is who we are as a team. It’s the constant across all communications and everything we create.

Voice expresses our personality. Anyone who engages with something we create should be able to identify it as distinctly the Learning Team.

Tone is adaptable and flexible. Think of voice as the umbrella over tone. While voice is always consistent, tone changes depending on the audience and scenario.

Use tone to your advantage – it’s here to cultivate specific feelings in your audience.
